Swarmudra Auditions

Sadhya Bhatnagar

The dance society of GGSIPU, aptly named Swaramudra organized the second round of auditions for the academic session 2015-16 on the 17th of September, 2015. It was held in the D-Block Auditorium and a total of 40 participants, shortlisted from the first round, attempted to bring the stage to life. They were well managed to do so as the audience could be seen enjoying themselves to the fullest, clapping and cheering for their favourites.

The performances took place in groups of four or five, with each team having been assigned a team coordinator previously. These coordinators were existing members of the club. The auditorium was filled to the brim and the crowd was energetic and responsive which also helped the participants gain confidence on stage.

When asked about her experience, Shivani Gupta from USAP had the following words, “Participating in the team round of the dance auditions was a great experience. The concept of making random teams helped us bond with each other which made the practice sessions fun.” Post the successful event, the various participants were assigned their teams within the society based on their strengths.
