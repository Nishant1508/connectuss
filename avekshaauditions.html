“Aao, aao, Auditions dekho!”

Aashli Pathni

Aveksha, the Dramatics Society under the aegis of Srijan, recently held its auditions to select members for the session 2015-16. The auditions were held on 26th August 2015, in the D-Block auditorium. The event had been well-publicized in the week leading up to the auditions themselves, with posters and word of mouth flying around the students in the campus.

Come the day of the auditions themselves, the venue began crowing up an hour before the event was due to start, as an increasing number of interested students from various departments queued up to get their names registered. The event began as scheduled, and the auditorium was packed to the brim with theatre-hopefuls. To kick things off, the pre-existing members of Aveksha performed a chorus of their call song ‘Oye Aaja Aaja’, their synchronized voices echoing around the auditorium, in perfect tandem with the resounding thumps of their feet on the wooden stage, setting the mood for the afternoon. Following an introduction to the Street and Stage sub-societies and brief history of the society, the audition itself finally started.

The event received an overwhelming number of responses, with over a hundred people auditioning for a place in the society. Touching stories, emotional monologues, melodious voices, graceful postures and hilarious acts were showcased in quick succession, as each aspiring student came up on the platform to express their thoughts, beliefs and portray their talent, brimming with potential and promise. At the end of an exhausting day, the lists finally ended at 8 pm when the last person completed his audition.

Due to a crushing response, the members of Aveksha conducted a second round of auditions on Monday, 1st September. The meeting was conducted under the sun in the ground behind the E-block. The participants were guided through a grilling routine of physical exercises, acting workshops and fun games and discussions, with the aim of filtering the best and most committed of the new lot.

The two days of auditions finally paid off, as the Dramatics Society was joined by a bunch of fresh faces, and new people to take the society forward in its future ventures.
