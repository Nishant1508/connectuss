Power Lifting and Body Building

Anukriti Jain

“It's not about perfect. It's about effort. And when you bring that effort every single day, that's where transformation happens. That's how change occurs.”
-Jillian Michaels

This is the spirit which keeps the power lifters and body builders of IP University going even when they have to perform strenuous tasks. Their constant efforts and endeavours are taking them places. Some of the students selected for body building and power lifting will be representing our University in the All India Inter University Championship scheduled from 5th January to 15th January at Punjab Technical University, Jalandhar

We hope that they perform well at the national level and bring laurels to the University. The support of the entire University is with them.
