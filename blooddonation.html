BLOOD DONATION DRIVE @ INDRAPRASTHA UNIVERSITY CAMPUS, DWARKA

Geetanjali

It was on a Tuesday, I read the poster regarding a Blood donation drive by Rotary Club Bank at the main entrance gate. “Donating blood is a divine experience”, it read. I was filled with an excitement so over-whelming that I decided to go for it this time, without caring about my classes. What transpired after this, left me numb, speechless and feeling really good! After getting myself registered, all I had to do was wait for the haemoglobin and blood pressure check-up and I must confess that the queue comprised equally of teachers and students!

To put in words, hygiene didn’t meet the primary criterion for success; it was, in fact beyond success! All the medical equipment being used was new in state and the doctors made sure that the donor leaving the bed wasn’t feeling weak at all.

This drive was a huge success and was conducted for three extra hours after the registrations were closed as it was met with great enthusiasm campus-wide. The donors were rewarded with refreshments, a certificate and on-the-spot donor card (without any delay), valid for them and their families’ use in emergency cases.
