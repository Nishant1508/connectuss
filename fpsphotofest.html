FPS Photofest 2015

Anil Kapoor

The Film and Photography Society (FPS) of GGSIPU hosted an exhibition of photographs from 7th – 14th September. The exhibition was inaugurated by Prof. Seyed E. Hasnain and among the Guest of Honour were Vice Chancellor Prof. Anil K. Tyagi and Dr. Anup Beniwal, Dean, USMC

The exhibitors were members of the society from USMC, USEM and USHSS. FPS also held a photography competition for which the themes were ‘Seasons of the campus’ and ‘Common ground’.

The event also included a workshop by renowned Shri. Ashish Bhattacharjee, senior instructor of photography at Bal Bhawan, who explained the students the aesthetics and composition in photography and what makes a good photograph. He also shared some of his interesting experiences in the field.

Two other workshops were held on the last day of exhibition. The first one was by acclaimed cinematographer and photographer, Mr Sanjay Agarwal. Explaining through his hand movements, he made the complex aspects of photography like shutter and aperture, which made the concepts extremely easy to understand and to retain. Mr Agarwal also explained different lighting techniques by displaying some of his photographs.

The other workshop was conducted by Mr Kamal Narang, photo editor of the distinguished newspaper, ‘The Hindu’. He captured the students’ interest by talking about various aspects in news photography and ‘what makes a photograph news worthy’.

The competition was judged by Mr Sanjay Agarwal and Mr Kamal Narang. According to them, it was a tough choice. Among the 30 participants for both the categories, 1st prize went to Pipas Kumar, USEM, 2nd prize went to Sangeeta Dayal, USMC and consolation prizes were given to Aanchal Mathur, USMC and Ashima Kohli, USHSS respectively.

Special Mention was given to Mr Shishupal Kumar, a student and a professor at USMC.
A token of participation along with certificates were given to all those who participated in the exhibition as well as the competition.
